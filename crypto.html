Cryptography


Cryptography is the process of where you encode a message and then someone you send that encoded message to decodes the message. There are many different kinds of encoding ciphers. Caesar Cipher, also known as rotation cipher, is one of the most well-known cipher in the world. A tool I recommend using that has all sorts of different kinds of ciphers is CyberChef.
